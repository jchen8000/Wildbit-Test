Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. All plans are billed monthly and you can cancel or downgrade at any time. If you cancel, you’ll only be charged for the current billing period.

What happens when my 14 day trial expires?

We'll email you 2 days before to let you know your trial is expiring. You don't need to do anything to keep using Beanstalk, we'll just charge your card on the 15th day. If you want to cancel or downgrade, you can do so in just a few clicks.

Can I try Beanstalk with the free plan?

We have a free account for independents offering 1 user and 1 repository. No credit card is required. If you are testing with a team we recommend the full trial which allows inviting unlimited users.

Do you offer more storage?

Yes. We can increase the storage beyond our highest plan. Get in touch and we’ll make it happen.

What types of payments do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard and American Express. We do not accept PayPal, checks or purchase orders.

Can I pay annually?

Yes. You can make one time payments to cover six months, one year or more, making it ideal for expense reports.
